Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 11, 2026

1. Information Collection

We highly value your privacy protection. When using our product, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: When registering, we collect your email address and password for account management and identity verification.
  • Note Content: Your notes are stored locally on your device or encrypted when synced to the cloud.
  • Device Information: To provide the best experience, we collect device model and OS version information.

2. Information Usage

We only use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process your requests and feedback
  • Send important notifications (e.g., security updates)
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

3. Information Storage

Local Storage: Your notes are stored locally on your device by default, accessible only to you.

Cloud Storage: When cloud sync is enabled, data is transmitted via HTTPS encryption and securely stored on cloud servers.

4. Information Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • HTTPS encryption for data transmission
  • Encrypted storage for cloud data
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability fixes

5. Information Sharing

Except for the following situations, we will not share your personal information with any third party:

  • With your explicit consent
  • As required by law
  • To protect our legitimate rights and interests

6. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view your note data at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all data at any time
  • Export: You can export all your note data

7. Minors Protection

We highly value the protection of minors' personal information. If you are a minor, please use our services under guardian supervision.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updated policies will be communicated through in-app notifications or email.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the in-app feedback feature or via email.
